Admission Procedure for 2025-28

BCA admission 2025 and BBA admission 2025 started at CIMAGE College Patna. Other courses like BBM, BBA, B.com and PGDM admission are also started for 2025-28 session. At CIMAGE, admission is granted through ‘Entrance cum Scholarship Test’, followed by ‘One to one’ Personal Interview. Those, who qualify the mentioned rounds below, are given the chance to take admission at CIMAGE. To get admission for 2025-28 session you can call at 7250767676 or fill the enquiry form at our website www.cimage.in. The counsellors will get back to you and provide you all the information regarding admission in BCA, BBA, BBM, B.Sc.IT or PGDM courses for 2025 session.

Economical weaker student can opt the facility of Student credit card issued by Bihar government to get admission at CIMAGE group of Institution. It is one of the best colleges under Aryabhatta Knowledge University and Patliputra University. For the admission of BCA and BBA courses, CIMAGE College is the best college as it is offering world class and modern education environment with campus placement facility. Getting admission in CIMAGE is the best opportunity for a student who wants to make a good career.
